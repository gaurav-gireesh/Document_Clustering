but may the fate of Constans serve as a warning to the
persecutors of the church. After his just condemnation
by the bishops of Sicily, the tyrant was cut off, in the
fulness of his sins, by a domestic servant; the saint is
still adored by the nations of Scythia, among whom he
ended his banishment and his life. But it is our duty
to live for the edification and support of the faithful
people; nor are we reduced to risk our safety on the
event of a combat. Incapable as you are of defending
your Roman subjects, the maritime situation of the city
may perhaps expose it to your depredation; but we
