11. Hyrcanus sent also one of these ambassadors to Dolabella,
who was then the prefect of Asia, and desired him to dismiss
the Jews from military services, and to preserve to them the
customs of their forefathers, and to permit them to live ac­
cording to them. And when Dolabella had received Hyrcan­
us' letter, without any farther deliberation, he sent an epistle
