his righteousness, and was in great reputation in Jerusalem, the
greatest city we have. I was myself brought up with my bro­
ther, whose name was Matthias, for he was my own brother,
by both father and mother ; and I made mighty proficiency in
the improvements of my learning, and appeared to have both
