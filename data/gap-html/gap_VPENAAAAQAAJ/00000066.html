stain on the memory of Theodora. Her numerous
spies observed, and zealously reported, every action, or
word, or look, injurious to their royal mistress. Whom­
soever they accused were cast into her peculiar prisons,1'
inaccessible to the inquiries of justice; and it was ru­

unhappy victims perished in deep unwholesome dun­
