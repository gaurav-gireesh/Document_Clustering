done both by the Greeks and Barbarians, under every one
of the Tyrian kings, and had taken much pains to learn their
history out of their own records. Now when he was writing
about those kings that had reigned at Tyre, he came to
Hirom, and says thus: — "Upon the death of Abibalus, his
son Hirom took the kingdom ; he lived fifty-three years, and
