of his age and country. The hatred of the Christians,
the love of spoil, and the contempt of danger, were the
ruling passion of the audacious Saracen; and the pros­
pect of instant death could never shake his religious con­
fidence, or ruffle the calmness of his resolution, or even
suspend the frank and martial pleasantry of his humour.
