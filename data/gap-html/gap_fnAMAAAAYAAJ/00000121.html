Eurystheus indeed, whose mother was the sister of Atreus,

therefore the return of Eurystheus was prevented by death,
and the Myceneans from a dread of the Heraclida e were well

the affections of the people, he easily obtained the kingdom
of Mycenae and all the territories which had belonged to
Eurystheus ; and from hence the family of Pelops quite
overpowered the family of Perseus. To these enlargements
of power Agamemnon succeeding, and being also superior
to the rest of his countrymen in naval strength, he was
enabled in my opinion to form that expedition more from
