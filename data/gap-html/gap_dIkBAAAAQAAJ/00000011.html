PREFATORY NOTE.

The changes, which the Author has had occasion to make in
the new edition of the third and fourth volumes of this
work, have chiefly arisen out of the recent discovery of the
Fragments of Licinianus, which have supplemented our
defective information as to the epoch from the battle of
Pydna to the revolt of Lepidus in various not unimportant
points, but have also suggested various fresh difficulties.
