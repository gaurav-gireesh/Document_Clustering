LVIII. About the same time Vitellius
received advices that the two Mauri-
tanias (a) had acceded to his party. This
event was occasioned by the murder of
Luceius Albinus, the governor of that
country. The province which was called
Caesariensis had been by Nero committed
to Albinus; and the other, called Tingi-
tana, was afterwards added by Galba.
