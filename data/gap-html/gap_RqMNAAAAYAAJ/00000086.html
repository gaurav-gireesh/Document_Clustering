on his march towards Capua, met the consul Claudius

forty thousand foot, and two thousand five hundred
horse, with which the consul was to act against Han­
nibal. The rest of the forces Hostilius was ordered
to lead to Capua, that he might deliver them up to
Quintus Fulvius, proconsul.
