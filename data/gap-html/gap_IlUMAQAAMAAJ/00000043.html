been the first of the Romans. To form a body of
nobles, whose influence may restrain, while it se­

very inconsistent with the character and policy of
Constantine; but had he seriously entertained such
a design, it might have exceeded the measure of his
power to ratify, by an arbitrary edict, an institution
which must expect the sanction of time and of opinion.

he revived it . as a personal, not as an hereditary
