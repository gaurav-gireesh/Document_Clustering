The inscription of the stupendous bridge of Alcantara, attests that

tanian communities. When Pliny was intrusted with the govern­

diction striving with each other in every useful and ornamental
work, that might deserve the curiosity of strangers, or the gratitude
of their citizens. It was the duty of the Proconsul to supply their
deficiencies, to direct their taste, and sometimes to moderate their
emulation 66. The opulent senators of Rome and1 the provinces
