Next day, the prisoners were disposed of by lot, one
being assigned to each of the knights and centurions,
and to such as had distinguished themselves more parti­
cularly, two a-piece. The rest were sold to the highest
bidder: and then the dictator led back his artny, crown­
ed with laurels, and enriched with plunder, and entered
the city in triumph.

He then ordered the general of the horse to resign his
office, and abdicated his own the sixteenth day after he
had received it; leaving the city in the most profound
peace and tranquillity, which he had found involved in
war, and in extreme consternation.
