magazines of corn, of arms, and of splendid furniture,
were partly distributed among the troops, and partly
reserved for the public service: the useless stores
were destroyed by fire, or thrown into the stream of
the Euphrates; and the fate of Amida was revenged
by the total ruin of Perisabor.
