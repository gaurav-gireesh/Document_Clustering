(a) M. Valerius Trachalus was joint consul with

(b) Gtho left the city of Rome on the 24th day of
March, as appears from Suetonius, who mentions his
neglect of the institutions relating to the Ancilia, as an
inauspicious beginning of the war. Suetoniu3 adds,
that he set out on the day when all who paid their
worship to the mother of the gods began the usual
ceremonies. Now that day was the gth of the kalends
of April, which answers to the 24th of March. See
Suetonius, Life of Otho, s. 8.
