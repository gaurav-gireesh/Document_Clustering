the stirrup, when he heard the voice of a suppliant,—
O son of the uncle of the apostle of God, I am a travel­

the pilgrim with his camel, her rich caparison, and a
purse of four thousand pieces of gold, excepting only
the sword, either for its intrinsic value, or as the gift of
an honoured kinsman. The servant of Kais informed
