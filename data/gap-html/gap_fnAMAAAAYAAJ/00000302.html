Twenty ships were the number they agfee to send him,

friend of the Athenians, had persuaded them to appear be­
fore Cydonia, assuring them that this place, which had been
an enemy to them, should soon be their own. This he in­

upon the Cydonians. The commander therefore with these

bly wasted away.
