Had the Mahometans been united, Italy must have
fallen an easy and glorious accession to the empire of
the Prophet. But the Caliphs of Bagdad had lost
their authority in the West; the Aglabites and Fa-

of Sicily aspired to independence; and the design of
conquest and dominion was degraded to a repetition
of predatory inroads.
