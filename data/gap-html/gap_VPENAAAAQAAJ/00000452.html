Lamenting the barren superfluity of materials, I have
studied to compress the narrative of these uninteresting
transactions: but the just Nushirvan is still applauded
as the model of oriental kings, and the ambition of his
grandson Chosroes prepared the revolution of the east,
which was speedily accomplished by the arms and the
religion of the successors of Mahomet.
