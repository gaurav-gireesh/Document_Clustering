Thrace. He found Rhescuporis in a,state of violent

hazard his person within the lines of a Roman garri­
son. Pretending there to do honour to the prince, he
appointed a guard to attend him. The tribunes and
centurions enticed him to go forward under their pro­
tection; till having drawn him a considerable way,
they avowed their purpose, and Rhescuporis found
