way, from Capua to the neighbourhood of Rome. The
forces that arrived by land and sea were united at the
mouth of the Tiber. Antonina convened a council of
war: it was resolved to surmount, with sails and oars,,
the adverse stream of the river: and the Goths were
apprehensive of disturbing, by any rash hostilities, the
negotiation to which Belisarius had craftily listened.
