peace which betrayed the common freedom of mankind,
that prince, your ally in words, your enemy in actions,
has alike insulted his friends and foes, and has filled the
earth with blood and confusion. Has he not violated
the privileges of Armenia, the independence of Colchos,
and the wild liberty of the Tzanian mountains? Has
