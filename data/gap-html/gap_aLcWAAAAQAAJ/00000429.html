Their forms were various, their proportions just, but they were

neither windows nor chimnies. They were lighted from the

story), and they received their heat by the help of pipes that were
conveyed along the walls. The range of principal apartments'
was protected towards the south west, by a portico five hundred and
seventeen feet long, which must have formed a very noble and
delightful walk, when the beauties of painting and sculpture were
added to those of the prospect.
