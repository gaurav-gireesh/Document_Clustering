30. Our nation, therefore, according to Manctho, was not
derived from Egypt, nor were any of the Egyptians mingled
with us. For it is to be supposed that many of the leprous
and distempered people were dead in the mines, since they
had been there a long time, and in so ill a condition ; many
others must be dead in the battles that happened afterward,
and more still in the last battle and flight after it.
