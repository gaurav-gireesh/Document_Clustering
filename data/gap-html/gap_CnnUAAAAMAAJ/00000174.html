How, upon Antiochus' prohibition to the Jews to make use of the
laws of their country, Mattathias the son of Asmoneus, alone
despised the king, and overcame the generals of Antiochus'
army; as also concerning the death of Mattathias, and the
succession of Judas.

to live so ingloriously as they then did.
