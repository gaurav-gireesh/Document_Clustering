that their pretor Fanius should give them money out of the
public treasury to bear their expenses home. And thus did
Fanius dismiss the Jewish ambassadors, and gave them money
out of the public treasury; and gave the decree of the senate
to those that were to conduct them, and take care that they
should return home in safety.
