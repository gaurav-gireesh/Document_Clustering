The ceremony of his abdication was performed in a spacious

plain, about three miles from Nicomedia. The emperor ascended a

lofty throne, and in a speech, full of reason and dignity, declared his

intention, both to the people and to the soldiers who were assembled

on this extraordinary occasion. As soon as he had divested himself of 3°S*

the purple, he withdrew from the gazing multitude; and traversing
