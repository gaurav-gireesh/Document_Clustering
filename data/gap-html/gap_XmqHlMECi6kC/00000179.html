Maria was strangled, her corpse was buried in the sea,
and her memory was wounded by the insult most offen­
sive to female vanity, a false and ugly representation of
her beauteous form. The fate of her son was not long
deferred: he was strangled with a bowstring, and the
tyrant, insensible to pity or remorse, after surveying the
body of the innocent youth, struck it rudely with his
foot:—Thy father (he cried) was a knave, thy mother a
whore, and thyself a fool!
